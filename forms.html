Scholar Life

With our Blended Learning system, pupils get to learn and experience education at a new level.

Diversity and Social inclusion are among our major objectives as a school.

Clubs and Activities

Music

Our Pupils enjoy a diversity of Classical Music lessons together with diverse African traditional music.

Writing

Our Pupils are involved weekly in creative writing both in Swahili and English where they cover every issue arising in their communities.

Dance

Salsa, Sengenya, Mdundiko, Ohangla, Ramogi, Isikuti, Kumabeka are among our elite dancing styles during our weekly school dances.

Future Students

Tiwi School admits new pupils every term. Our school opens every Monday and Tuesdays during Holidays for interviews of all incoming students.

Current Students

Tiwi School is a community existing together under one umbrella, students are taught not only to be knowledgeble but also to be good ambasadors of their school and also their culture.

Alumni Students

All our grade 8 graduates make it to high school. Tiwi schools boasts the highest number of female university students in session since 1998.
